Pool Parties

Captain Obvious once defined this event as a party in a pool. The truth is that it usually consists on just that, and we say usually because we know from experience that this kind of meetings are unpredictable; we have been holding them for ages in SeleneDiccy's Realm, so we know what we are talking about. It is said that the first Pool Party ever was a distraction Gexgekko created to lure people from his realm (where he didn't want anybody) to his sister's; from that day on random people started asking for more Pool Parties in the her amazing Pool, which she had to build specially for that purpose. It is a very easy event to organize since all you need is a pool (there is one in The Killers of Parted Souls Clan realm) and a bunch of people, so if you feel like having a crazy meeting at any given time just tell us and we will help spread the word.
